  • What do we do?

    • A website that allows people to buy clothes for people through retail clothing companies.

    Once you shop for yourself, you can click on a link that directs you to a list of profiles of people in need of clothes. The donator can read a description of a variety of people and choose who to shop for.

    If you donate, you recieve a discount from your current order and help someone in need.

    Donation options are filtered throughout people in need. After a person recieves a donation (clothes) they are bumped from the priority list.
